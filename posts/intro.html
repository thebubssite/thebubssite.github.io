Introduction

What am I writing about? Why am I writing about it? Who's meant to read this?

Posted on June 19, 2018

Wow my first post. I took this template from Bootstrap and thought I would have a lot more sections for posts split into categories such as pingpong, computer science, and maybe just miscellaneous thoughts about life. But as I don’t have so many posts yet, I’ve decided just to have one section for everything. So maybe in the future, my website will undergo some more interesting changes. But for now, I think it’s best to keep it simple.

Why am I writing my thoughts? Well, mainly for myself. It’s nice to keep things organized on the internet and the fonts are certainly more readable than my own handwriting. And also, just for anyone who’s interested in hearing my thoughts or ideas. Even if no one reads anything, I think it’s pretty fun to imagine someone out there is reading my writing and it’s a good motivational tool to keep updating my website. But for the most part, I want to see how my thoughts develop over time.

I’m going to be trying to write more about my journey through computer science as well as some random thoughts about life more than I will write about pingpong. Pingpong used to be a really big part of my life, but it’s taken a backseat to school and other interests I've been exploring. But yeah, maybe I’ll start my blog topics with the CS courses I’ve taken at Berkeley. Write about what I’m trying to accomplish with my life and different thoughts I’ve heard from what others are trying to accomplish. How working at Illumio is. Things that make me sad and things that make me happy. Cool events that happen. I don’t know. Stuff like that.

Anyways, I hope it turns out great!
