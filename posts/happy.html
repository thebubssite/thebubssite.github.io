Things that Make Me Happy

Yay!

Posted on July 16, 2018

In no particular order, these are things that make me happy and smile. At least on the inside because I’m not a very expressive person. But I’m trying to be.

1) Strawberries that are sweet and not sour.

2) Being on a Macbook Pro 2015. It’s such a nice laptop. But my normal laptop is Windows/Ubuntu I cry

3) Friends who are comfortable with silence and don’t have to fill up every moment with meaningless words. I like people whose words mean something.

4) Rewatching the Office or Parks and Rec for the billionth time. It’s comforting. I just started watching The Good Place though, and it’s great!

5) Using keyboard shortcuts while coding. Makes me feel like hackerman. Too bad I don’t know many shortcuts.

6) Unlikely animal friendships.

7) When I see a new dog who’s super excited to see me.

8) Lobbing in pingpong, and then I make a crazy counterloop and it turns into an awesome point.

9) Meeting someone whose very confident in their unorthodox path.

10) Seeing Rubinder Singh’s wardrobe choices every day at work. This man dresses on point. So classy. So stylish.
